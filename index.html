    Our Story

    We're Cat Lovers Too

    Toby Bag was born from a simple idea: cat lovers deserve bags that celebrate their passion. We searched high and low to curate the most adorable, high-quality cat-themed bags available.

    Named after Toby — a very opinionated orange tabby who approves of every bag before it makes it to our store — we bring you only the best cat-themed totes, backpacks and more.

    Every product in our collection is handpicked for quality, design and that special something that makes cat lovers smile. All purchases are fulfilled safely and securely through Amazon.
